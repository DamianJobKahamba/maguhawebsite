About Mr. Maguha Nimilwa

8+ Years Teaching

I hold a BSc in Physics and a Postgraduate Certificate in Education (PGCE). My journey into teaching began with a simple belief: every student can grasp physics when it is taught with patience, clarity, and real-world relevance.

I currently teach at secondary school and A-level, covering topics from Newtonian mechanics and thermodynamics to electricity, waves, and introductory quantum physics. Outside of school, I run private tutoring sessions and produce free educational content on YouTube.
